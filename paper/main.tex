\begin{abstract}
Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) remains a leading cause of preventable blindness globally. We introduce a comprehensive, production-ready DR Intelligence Platform that combines ensemble classification models (EfficientNet, Vision Transformers), advanced lesion segmentation networks, and an explainable AI module for predicting disease progression. Our system achieves state-of-the-art calibration and performance on benchmark datasets including EyePACS, APTOS 2019, and Messidor-2.
\end{abstract}
